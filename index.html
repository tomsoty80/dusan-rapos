Biography

Dušan Rapoš (born 20 June 1953, Moravany) is a Slovak film director, screenwriter and composer. He studied journalism at Comenius University and film directing at the Academy of Performing Arts in Bratislava (VŠMU). Best known for the music-driven Fontána pre Zuzanu films, his work spans drama, comedy, documentary and family titles. He is married to Czech actress Eva Vejmělková.
