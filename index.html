Biography

Dušan Rapoš (born 20 June 1953, Moravany, former Czechoslovakia) is a Slovak film director, screenwriter and composer. He studied journalism at Comenius University and film directing at the Academy of Performing Arts in Bratislava (VŠMU). He has worked across narrative features, music-driven films, documentaries and TV, and is widely known for the Fontána pre Zuzanu trilogy. He is married to Czech actress Eva Vejmělková.

Sources: Wikipedia (EN/SK), ČSFD profiles.
